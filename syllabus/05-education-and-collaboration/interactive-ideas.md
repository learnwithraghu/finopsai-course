# Interactive HTML Concepts

## 1. Enablement Plan Optimizer
- **Tough concept addressed**: Turning generic training checklists into measurable adoption programs.
- **Experience flow**:
  1. Users input team sizes, maturity levels, and desired behaviors through structured forms.
  2. A simulation engine projects engagement over time, visualizing adoption curves and resource load via area charts.
  3. The session ends with recommended interventions (office hours, labs) ranked by expected ROI, giving the "wow" moment of data-driven enablement.
- **Learning punchline**: Tailored enablement plans are measurable and iterative, not static slide decks.

## 2. Persona Alignment Simulator
- **Tough concept addressed**: Quantifying soft-skill collaboration work.
- **Experience flow**:
  1. Begin with disconnected persona nodes (finance, product, ML, procurement) on a graph.
  2. Users schedule virtual touchpoints (steering committee, office hours, shared channel) via timeline inputs; edges strengthen as meetings occur.
  3. The system outputs collaboration health scores and identifies neglected relationships, showing how intentional rituals reduce friction.
- **Learning punchline**: Consistent rituals and messaging are the backbone of multi-team FinOps success.
