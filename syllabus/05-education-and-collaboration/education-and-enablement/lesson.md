# Education & Enablement

## Purpose
Equip cross-functional teams with the knowledge and tooling needed to act on AI FinOps insights.

## Learning Objectives
- Design enablement programs tailored to executives, platform teams, and data scientists
- Create reusable collateral (playbooks, office hours, labs) that reinforce desired behaviors
- Measure enablement effectiveness using engagement and adoption metrics

## Discussion Topics
- Curriculum design and delivery mediums (live, async, labs)
- Communications strategy for reinforcing FinOps guardrails
- Feedback loops into product and process backlogs

## Practical Activities
- Draft an enablement calendar covering the next two quarters
- Build a starter deck that explains key AI FinOps controls
- Define evaluation methods (surveys, adoption KPIs, certification targets)

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Enablement calendar
- Training deck outline
- Adoption KPI dashboard requirements
