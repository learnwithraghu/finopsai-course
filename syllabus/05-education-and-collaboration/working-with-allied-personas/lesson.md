# Working With Allied Personas

## Purpose
Deepen collaboration between FinOps, finance, procurement, data science, and platform teams to accelerate AI outcomes.

## Learning Objectives
- Map persona-specific goals and pain points across the AI delivery chain
- Define engagement cadences such as steering forums, backlog grooming, and office hours
- Codify decision rights to avoid bottlenecks when managing AI spend

## Discussion Topics
- Persona empathy maps (finance, procurement, ML engineering, product)
- Collaboration touchpoints and tooling (shared notebooks, dashboards, chat channels)
- Conflict resolution patterns and escalation ladders

## Practical Activities
- Conduct a persona workshop to capture needs, metrics, and preferred communication styles
- Draft a RACI for AI cost decisions (allocations, commitments, experimentation limits)
- Evaluate current forums and propose improvements to cadence and agenda

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Persona canvases
- Decision RACI matrix
- Updated forum charter
