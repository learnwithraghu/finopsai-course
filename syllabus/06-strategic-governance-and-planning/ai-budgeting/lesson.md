# AI Budgeting

## Purpose
Connect AI program forecasts to funding models and budget accountability.

## Learning Objectives
- Design budget structures that differentiate innovation, training, and production inference
- Align FinOps budgeting cycles with corporate FP&A timelines
- Set guardrails for budget consumption and reforecast triggers

## Discussion Topics
- Funding models: centralized platforms vs. chargeback to product lines
- Capex vs. opex considerations for on-prem accelerators
- Escalation paths when spend exceeds guardrails

## Practical Activities
- Map current budgets to AI use cases and identify gaps
- Define budget monitoring dashboards and alert thresholds
- Document reforecast scenarios and approval workflows

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Budget allocation model
- Monitoring dashboard requirements
- Reforecast SOP
