# AI Planning & Estimating

## Purpose
Equip teams with methods to estimate AI program costs before large commitments are made.

## Learning Objectives
- Translate AI roadmaps into capacity plans for compute, storage, and talent
- Apply estimation techniques such as analogous models, parametric scaling, and pilot burn extrapolation
- Integrate FinOps planners with finance budgeting cycles and PMO gates

## Discussion Topics
- Input drivers: model size, epochs, dataset scale, inference latency targets
- Scenario planning for best/expected/worst usage cases
- Handshakes with procurement and finance for lead times

## Practical Activities
- Build an estimation sheet for an upcoming AI initiative using historical baselines
- Review assumptions with engineering leads and track risk factors
- Create a decision log capturing estimate revisions and approval history

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Estimation workbook
- Risk register
- Decision log
