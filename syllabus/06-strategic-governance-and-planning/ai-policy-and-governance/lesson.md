# AI Policy & Governance

## Purpose
Define the guardrails, approvals, and compliance requirements that keep AI innovation aligned with enterprise policy.

## Learning Objectives
- Document AI financial guardrails covering experimentation spend, data residency, and sustainability goals
- Link FinOps governance mechanisms to existing risk, security, and compliance forums
- Measure adherence through policy exceptions, review cadences, and audit evidence

## Discussion Topics
- Policy categories: budgetary, architectural, procurement, data governance
- Governance bodies (AI councils, FinOps steering committee, architecture review boards)
- Control monitoring and exception management

## Practical Activities
- Draft or update AI cost governance policy statements
- Map policy controls to evidence sources (dashboards, tickets, approvals)
- Plan quarterly governance reviews with clear inputs and outputs

## Artifacts & Metrics
- AI governance charter
- Control-to-evidence matrix
- Quarterly review agenda
