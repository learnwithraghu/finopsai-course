# FinOps Certified: FinOps for AI — Syllabus

## Course Overview
FinOps Certified: FinOps for AI equips FinOps practitioners, platform engineers, and AI program leaders with an end-to-end playbook for governing AI spend. The syllabus aligns with the FinOps Framework while calling out the nuances of GPU-based training, accelerated inference, and AI-specific procurement models. Completion of all lessons prepares learners for the FinOps Certified: FinOps for AI credential.

## Structure
The curriculum is organized into seven sections. Each section folder contains subfolders for every lesson, and each lesson includes a dedicated `lesson.md` file.

1. `01-introduction-to-finops-for-ai`
2. `02-ai-cost-allocation`
3. `03-data-sources-and-reporting`
4. `04-anomaly-detection-and-invoicing`
5. `05-education-and-collaboration`
6. `06-strategic-governance-and-planning`
7. `07-advanced-optimization-and-certification`

## Recommended Prerequisites
- Working knowledge of the FinOps Framework (FinOps Certified Practitioner or Engineer recommended)
- Familiarity with AI/ML workloads, including training vs. inference lifecycles
- Access to organizational cloud billing data for applied exercises

## Certification Details
- Exam eligibility: complete all lessons across Sections 1–7
- Credential validity: 24 months with digital badge and certificate issued upon passing
- Assessment coverage: scenario-based questions spanning visibility, allocation, governance, optimization, and KPI reporting for AI workloads

## How to Use This Syllabus
- Navigate each section folder sequentially; lessons build from foundational visibility to advanced optimization
- Use the objectives and discussion prompts in every `lesson.md` to facilitate workshops or self-study
- Capture implementation decisions, KPIs, and action items alongside the provided prompts to tailor the material to your organization
- Leverage the demo scenarios in `demos/README.md` to reinforce each section with live or recorded walkthroughs
- Reference `interactive-ideas.md` in each section for advanced single-page HTML experiences that transform complex concepts into hands-on simulations
