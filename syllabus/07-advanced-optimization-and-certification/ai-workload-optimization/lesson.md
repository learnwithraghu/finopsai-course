# AI Workload Optimization

## Purpose
Optimize AI workloads through rightsizing, scheduling, and feature-level tuning to improve efficiency.

## Learning Objectives
- Identify inefficiencies across training pipelines, feature stores, and inference endpoints
- Apply GPU scheduling tactics such as queue prioritization and mixed precision training
- Quantify optimization impact using business KPIs and cost savings

## Discussion Topics
- Rightsizing model architectures and dataset sampling
- Job orchestration and queue management
- Observability for utilization, throughput, and error budgets

## Practical Activities
- Review pipeline telemetry to flag low-utilization jobs
- Experiment with optimization techniques (checkpointing, pruning) and log results
- Create an optimization backlog prioritized by ROI

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Optimization target list
- Experiment log
- Savings tracker
