# AI Rate Optimization

## Purpose
Secure favorable pricing for AI services through commitments, marketplace evaluations, and vendor negotiations.

## Learning Objectives
- Compare pricing models (on-demand, reserved, spot, dedicated clusters, SaaS tiers)
- Determine commitment strategies that balance flexibility with savings
- Partner with procurement to negotiate AI contracts and SLAs

## Discussion Topics
- Rate optimization levers: committed use, savings plans, private pricing, multi-vendor leverage
- Marketplace vs. direct contracts
- Tracking realized savings and avoided costs

## Practical Activities
- Build a commitment coverage dashboard for AI workloads
- Evaluate vendor proposals and highlight trade-offs
- Document negotiation strategy with walk-away points and value drivers

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Commitment coverage report
- Vendor evaluation matrix
- Negotiation brief
