# Licensing & SaaS Decisions

## Purpose
Evaluate third-party AI SaaS and licensing models to ensure value and compliance.

## Learning Objectives
- Assess per-seat, per-token, and usage-tier licensing options for AI platforms
- Integrate SaaS telemetry into FinOps reporting to monitor adoption vs. contracted units
- Establish renewal decision frameworks with product owners and procurement

## Discussion Topics
- Licensing metrics (users, tokens, documents, workloads)
- Shadow SaaS discovery and rationalization
- Renewal playbooks and exit strategies

## Practical Activities
- Inventory AI SaaS tools and map to business capabilities
- Compare contract terms and identify optimization levers
- Create a renewal readiness checklist 90 days prior to contract end

## Artifacts & Metrics
- AI SaaS inventory
- Contract comparison sheet
- Renewal readiness checklist
