# Unit Economics for AI Workloads

## Purpose
Link AI spend to business outcomes using unit cost models that inform pricing and prioritization.

## Learning Objectives
- Define meaningful units (per 1K tokens, per inference, per customer interaction)
- Allocate shared costs to units without double counting
- Use unit cost trends to drive product decisions and go/no-go evaluations

## Discussion Topics
- Selecting units aligned with value streams
- Cost driver trees and sensitivity analysis
- Communicating unit economics to executives and product teams

## Practical Activities
- Build a cost driver tree for a representative AI product
- Calculate current unit costs and compare to target margins
- Highlight levers that improve unit economics (optimization, pricing, packaging)

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Unit cost model
- Variance commentary
- Action plan for unit improvements
