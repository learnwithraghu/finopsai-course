# Interactive HTML Concepts

## 1. Optimization ROI Laboratory
- **Tough concept addressed**: Connecting low-level tuning tweaks to executive-level savings.
- **Experience flow**:
  1. Users configure a training workload (model size, precision, instance type) and kick off a virtual run.
  2. A twin simulation compares baseline vs. optimized variants (mixed precision, spot fleets, autoscaling). Charts plot runtime, cost, and utilization deltas in real time.
  3. The lab ends with a cumulative savings meter and storytelling tips for business reviews.
- **Learning punchline**: Optimization choices are quantifiable levers that finance leaders can champion.

## 2. Unit Economics Storyteller
- **Tough concept addressed**: Translating infra spend into per-unit profitability conversations.
- **Experience flow**:
  1. Begin with raw cloud cost buckets and business volume metrics. Users assign drivers (tokens, sessions, customers) via structured inputs.
  2. A dynamic tree map reveals cost contribution per unit; sensitivity sliders show how pricing or efficiency shifts affect margins.
  3. The experience closes with an auto-generated narrative explaining which levers improve unit economics, ready for exec consumption.
- **Learning punchline**: Unit economics make FinOps insights actionable for product strategy and pricing.
