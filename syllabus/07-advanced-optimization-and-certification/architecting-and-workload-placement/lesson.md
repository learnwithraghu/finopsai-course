# Architecting & Workload Placement

## Purpose
Guide architects on placing AI workloads across regions, clusters, and providers for cost, performance, and risk balance.

## Learning Objectives
- Evaluate trade-offs between managed AI services, cloud GPUs, on-prem accelerators, and hybrid options
- Incorporate sustainability, latency, and data sovereignty requirements into placement decisions
- Document decision frameworks that FinOps can review for cost implications

## Discussion Topics
- Reference architectures for training, tuning, and inference
- Capacity planning for GPU clusters and auto-scaling strategies
- Workload placement scorecards (cost, risk, compliance, proximity)

## Practical Activities
- Build a workload placement matrix for current AI services
- Perform a total cost of ownership comparison across deployment options
- Capture architecture decision records (ADRs) highlighting FinOps inputs

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Placement matrix
- TCO comparison sheet
- ADRs with FinOps review notes
