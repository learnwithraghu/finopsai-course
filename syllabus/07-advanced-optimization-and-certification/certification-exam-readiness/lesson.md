# Certification Exam Readiness

## Purpose
Prepare learners to sit for the FinOps Certified: FinOps for AI exam with confidence.

## Learning Objectives
- Summarize key takeaways from each section and map them to exam domains
- Create a personalized study plan with practice questions and labs
- Clarify logistics: scheduling, retake policy, and maintaining certification

## Discussion Topics
- Exam blueprint and weighting
- Study resources (official materials, community groups, mock exams)
- Post-certification requirements and badge maintenance

## Practical Activities
- Complete a knowledge gap analysis referencing exam domains
- Schedule study sessions and peer review circles
- Draft a continuing education plan for the 24-month certification window

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Study plan
- Knowledge gap tracker
- Continuing education roadmap

- Official exam guide: https://www.finops.org/training-certification/
