# Organization-Wide Reporting

## Purpose
Deliver consistent AI cost and KPI reporting tailored to executives, finance, and engineering leaders.

## Learning Objectives
- Define role-based dashboards that link AI spend to business value metrics
- Create recurring storytelling formats for steering committees and product teams
- Ensure reporting reconciles with financial systems and highlights action-oriented insights

## Discussion Topics
- Audience-specific reporting (CFO, product GM, ML lead)
- KPIs: cost per training hour, cost per 1K tokens, GPU utilization, anomaly counts
- Narrative techniques: OKR alignment, KPI scorecards, decision logs

## Practical Activities
- Draft a dashboard wireframe for each primary persona
- Build a sample monthly business review pack referencing KPIs and trends
- Review data reconciliation checklist with finance to validate trust

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Reporting persona matrix
- MPR template
- Reconciliation checklist
