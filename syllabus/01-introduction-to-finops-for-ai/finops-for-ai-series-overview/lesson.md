# FinOps for AI Series Overview

## Purpose
Introduce the scope, audience, and outcomes of the FinOps for AI learning path so stakeholders align on expectations before diving into tactics.

## Learning Objectives
- Clarify how the FinOps Framework extends to AI workloads across planning, building, and running phases
- Contrast Level 1, 2, and 3 capabilities and the skills assessed in the certification exam
- Set success criteria for teams adopting FinOps for AI practices over the next 12 months

## Discussion Topics
- FinOps for AI maturity model and section-by-section preview
- Key personas (executive sponsors, FinOps practitioners, platform owners, ML leads)
- Certification logistics, timelines, and learning commitments

## Practical Activities
- Map existing FinOps capabilities to the AI maturity levels using a self-assessment worksheet
- Identify stakeholder groups and assign section ownership for the course rollout
- Draft a 90-day learning plan that sequences sections with ongoing AI initiatives

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Completed maturity self-assessment
- Stakeholder map and communication plan
- Learning plan with checkpoints and success metrics
