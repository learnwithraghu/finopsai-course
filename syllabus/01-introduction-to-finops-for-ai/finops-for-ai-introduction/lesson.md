# FinOps for AI Introduction

## Purpose
Explain why AI workloads introduce unique cost, data, and accountability challenges and how FinOps mitigates them.

## Learning Objectives
- Differentiate AI training, fine-tuning, and inference patterns from traditional cloud services
- Describe GPU procurement constraints, bursty demand, and cross-functional accountability gaps
- Highlight early warning signs of uncontrolled AI spend and FinOps levers to regain visibility

## Discussion Topics
- AI workload lifecycle and cost drivers (compute, storage, networking, licensing)
- Shadow AI and unmanaged experimentation risks
- Role of FinOps in enabling responsible AI innovation

## Practical Activities
- Create a swimlane diagram showing teams involved in a typical AI lifecycle
- List top three cost risks observed in current AI pilots and map FinOps responses
- Draft leadership talking points that link AI velocity with fiscal accountability

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Lifecycle swimlane diagram
- Risk-to-control matrix
- Executive briefing outline
