# AI Invoices

## Purpose
Demystify AI supplier invoices and ensure they reconcile with FinOps allocation and finance requirements.

## Learning Objectives
- Explain invoice structures for managed AI services, GPU leases, and marketplace models
- Establish a reconciliation process across procurement, FinOps, and accounting teams
- Identify optimizations such as commitment alignment or commercial renegotiations

## Discussion Topics
- Common line items: GPU time, accelerator credits, data egress, SaaS usage fees
- Invoice-to-ledger workflows and tooling integrations
- Variance analysis and remediation actions

## Practical Activities
- Annotate a sample invoice highlighting cost drivers and allocation tags
- Create a RACI for invoice approvals and dispute management
- Track commitments vs. actuals to flag underused contracts

## Artifacts & Metrics
- Invoice annotation guide
- Invoice reconciliation SOP
- Commitment utilization tracker
